Objectives

1. Implement and validate relevant laboratory (initially PacBio, Hi-C and multi tissue transcriptomics) and computational pipelines to ensure efficient sequencing, assembly and annotation of Eukaryotic Genomes.

2. Explore the implementation of complementary technologies (e.g. Oxford Nanopore) to improve the resulting genome quality and sequencing efficiency, where relevant.

3. Release resulting genomes to public databases listing species in process, completed (including status updates), collaborators derived from and links to resulting data.
